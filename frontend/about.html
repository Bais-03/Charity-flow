About Goods for Good

Learn about our mission to create a transparent and effective bridge between donors and communities in need.

Our Story

Born from a simple idea, built on a foundation of trust.

Goods for Good was founded in 2015 with a simple yet powerful observation: countless homes have unused items in good condition, while many communities across India face a constant scarcity of basic necessities. The problem wasn't a lack of generosity, but a lack of a reliable, transparent bridge to connect the two.


We built a platform to change that. By creating a system where donors can easily list items and NGOs can request specific needs, we've replaced guesswork with precision. We verify every donation and provide impact reports, ensuring donors know exactly how their contribution helps. Our journey has been about proving that small acts of kindness, when channeled effectively, can create a ripple effect of massive, positive change.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our work every day.

Our Mission

To create a seamless, trustworthy, and efficient platform that connects donors of physical goods with verified NGOs, ensuring that every item reaches its intended beneficiary and makes a tangible impact.

Our Vision

To foster a culture of conscious giving and resource redistribution, where no usable item goes to waste and every community in India has access to the essential resources it needs to thrive.

Our Journey & Impact

Milestones we've achieved together with you.

2015 - Inception

Goods for Good is founded in Mumbai with the goal of creating a digital marketplace for physical donations.

2017 - First Major Campaign

We launch our first large-scale campaign, "Project Warmth," collecting over 10,000 blankets for communities in the Himalayan regions.

2020 - Nationwide Expansion

Our network expands to cover 15 major cities, enabling us to support a wider range of NGOs and reach more donors.

2022 - Introducing Impact Reports

We launch our donor impact reporting feature, giving every donor a direct view of the change their items made.

2023 - Social Impact Award

We are honored with the prestigious 'Social Impact Award' for our innovative and transparent model.

Today & Beyond

With thousands of donors and hundreds of NGO partners, we continue to grow our mission and impact every day.
