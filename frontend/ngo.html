How to Become a Partner

Our process is simple and designed to ensure trust and transparency.

1
2
3
4

1. Mail Us Your Interest

Send an email to our team at **partnerships@goodsforgood.org** with your NGO's details and recent work.

2. Administrative Verification

Our admin team will carefully vet your organization to verify its authenticity and mission.

3. Receive Your Credentials

Once approved, we will provide you with secure login credentials for your NGO dashboard.

4. Post Needs & Receive Goods

Log in to post your specific needs, and we'll match them with item donations from our donors.
